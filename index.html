About

I am Sasu, a multidisciplinary artist and stylist based in Accra, Ghana. My work moves across styling, creative direction, set design, and image-making, with a focus on experimental and conceptual fashion.

I use clothing, space, texture, and composition to build looks and scenes that feel intentional, expressive, and character-driven. My practice sits between fashion and art, exploring how clothes can shape mood, identity, and the way a story is seen.
